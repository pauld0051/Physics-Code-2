Velocity and Speed Calculator

Calculate the velocity or speed an object is travelling, the distance it travelled or the time it took to travel.

Start by adding two of the known variables to calculate the third.

Select the things you know:

Please note that this calculator converts all inputs into SI or metric units (ms-1, m and s). The velocity of an object is given by the equation \(v = \frac{d}{t}\), where \(v\) is the velocity, \(d\) is the distance traveled, and \(t\) is the time it takes to travel that distance. Select the two variables that you know and calculate for the third. You can change your selection later if you want. This will help you become familiar with the processes of calculating velocity equations.

For more information about velocity and the equation \(v = \frac{d}{t}\), please visit our velocity equation page.
