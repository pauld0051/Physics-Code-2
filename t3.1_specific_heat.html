Please note that this calculator calculates the heat (Q), mass (m), specific heat capacity (c), or temperature change (ΔT) of a known substance. It uses the equation Q = mcΔT, where Q is the heat energy, m is the mass, c is the specific heat capacity, and ΔT is the temperature change. All inputs are converted into SI units (J, kg, and K) for calculation. If you want to calculate mixtures, you will need to go to the mixture calculator page. For more information about specific heat capacity and the equation used in this calculator, please visit our specific heat capacity page.
