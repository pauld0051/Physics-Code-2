Please note that this calculator converts all inputs into SI or metric units (Nm-2). The pressure exerted by an object is given by the equation \(P = \frac{F}{A}\), where \(P\) is the pressure in, \(F\) is the force exerted, and \(A\) is the area of the object. Select the two variables that you know and calculate for the third. You can change your selection later if you want. This will help you become familiar with the processes of calculating pressure equations.

For more information about pressure and the equation \(P = \frac{F}{A}\), please visit our pressure equation page.
