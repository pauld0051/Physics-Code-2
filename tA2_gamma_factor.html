Gamma Factor Calculator

Calculate the relativistic gamma factor (γ) for an object in motion, accounting for its velocity and the speed of light. Or calculate how fast an object would be travelling at a certain gamma factor.

Start by selecting what you know to calculate the unknown variable.

Equation

\begin{equation*} \Large \gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}}} \end{equation*}
