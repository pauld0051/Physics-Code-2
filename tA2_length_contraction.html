Length Contraction Calculator

Calculate the length contraction effect, where an object's length appears to be shorter when moving at near-light speed velocities compared to those at rest, according to the theory of special relativity. The calculator allows you to determine the amount of length contraction experienced by an object moving at a certain velocity relative to an observer, or the velocity required to achieve a certain amount of length contraction.

Begin by selecting the known variables to calculate the unknown quantity.

Equation

\begin{equation*} \Large \Delta L = \frac{\Delta L_0}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}}} \end{equation*}
