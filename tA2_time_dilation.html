Time Dilation Calculator

Calculate the time dilation effect, where time appears to pass slower for objects moving at high velocities compared to those at rest, according to the theory of special relativity. The calculator allows you to determine the amount of time dilation experienced by an object moving at a certain velocity relative to an observer, or the velocity required to achieve a certain amount of time dilation.

Begin by selecting the known variables to calculate the unknown quantity.

Equation

\begin{equation*} \Large \Delta t = \frac{\Delta t_0}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}}} \end{equation*}
